About

Welcome to the Train Ticket Reservation System. This system allows passengers to reserve train tickets online easily. Users can view train details, select their source and destination, and book tickets securely.

Features

  1. Online Ticket Booking
  2. Offline Ticket Reservation
  3. Check Train Availability
  4. View Source and Destination
  5. Affordable Ticket Prices
  6. Easy Passenger Registration
